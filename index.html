Absolut Cube

The Absolut Cube is a BLE beacon connected service device for restaurant guests and staff. Simply flip The Cube to your desired choice, The Cube then lights up to get attention of the waiter. Simple, interactive and efficient!

The big thee benefits

The Customer

Will feel at ease and can focus on the people that they are with instead of trying to catch the staffs attention.

The Staff

No more stress and make unecessary trips. They will know exactly when their table needs their service.

The Owner

By having a more smoother service will results in more tables being waited with new customers.
